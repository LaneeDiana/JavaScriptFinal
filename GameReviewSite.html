Kingdom Hearts 1

Released on: March 28, 2002

About the Game

Kingdom Hearts, released in 2002, is a action and fantasy RPG which blends characters from Disney and Final Fantasy. It also involves tons of worlds from the Disney series. The protagonist is a 12 year old child named Sora, who holds a magical weapon called the Keyblade, which helps him defeat the enemies called Heartless.

Game Review

I think Kingdom Hearts was a cute and fun game, but also challenging.
